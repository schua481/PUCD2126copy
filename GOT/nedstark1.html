• HOUSE STARK
  • Lord Eddard Stark (Sean Bean)


     
  • RELATION

  • He was the older brother of Benjen, Lyanna and the younger brother of Brandon Stark. He is the father of Robb, Sansa, Arya, Bran and Rickon by his wife, Catelyn Tully, and Jon Snow, who he raised as his bastard son.

     
  • ROLE

  • Eddard was the head of House Stark, the Lord of Winterfell, Lord Paramount and Warden of the North, and later Hand of the King to King Robert I Baratheon.




  • KEY EPISODES

  • Episode 2

    Having accepted his new role as the Hand of the King, Eddard leaves Winterfell with his daughters Sansa and Arya, while Catelyn stays behind to tend to Bran.


  • Episode 3

    Ned joins the King's Small Council at King's Landing, capital of the Seven Kingdoms, and learns just how poorly Westeros is being managed.


  • Episode 5

    Ned refuses to participate in Robert's plan to assassinate the pregnant Daenerys Targaryen, and resigns as Robert's Hand, angering him.

    Episode 6

    Eddard sentences Gregor to death and sends a message to Tywin Lannister, summoning him to a trial. Eddard decides to send Sansa, and Arya back to Winterfell. Eddard finds out that Joffrey is not Robert's biological son.

    Episode 7

    Eddard tells Cersei he knows she is involved with Jaime and none of her children are Robert's. A dying Robert names Eddard as the "Protector of the realm" to rule until Joffrey reaches the legal age.

    Episode 9

    In a public hearing, Eddard confesses to treason and publicly affirms Joffrey as rightful heir. However, rather than sentencing him to the Wall as had been negotiated, a vengeful Joffrey has him decapitated, while Cersei and other counselors object futilely. Sansa and Arya watch their father die.
